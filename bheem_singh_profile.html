About Me

I am Bheem Singh, an aerospace engineer and research scholar with expertise in hypersonic aerothermodynamics, plasma-assisted flow control, and computational modeling of non-equilibrium flows. My academic foundation includes an M.Tech from DIAT Pune and active research engagements in high-speed and multiphysics simulation environments.

I work extensively with CFD platforms such as ANSYS Fluent, STAR CCM+, OpenFOAM and numerical methods like finite volume and finite element techniques for solving compressible Navier–Stokes equations under high-enthalpy and chemically reacting conditions.

Research Interests

  • High-temperature and chemically reacting flows
  • Two-temperature models and vibrational non-equilibrium
  • Hypersonic re-entry aerothermodynamics
  • Plasma dynamics and electromagnetic-fluid interaction
  • Data-driven modeling using POD/DMD and ROM methods

Projects

  • VTOL UAV Design and Optimization
    Designed a tilt-rotor UAV for surveillance and multi-mission use; integrated CAD, FEA, and CFD tools for flight performance and structural integrity.
